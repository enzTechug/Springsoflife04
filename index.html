About Spring of Life Elderly Care

Spring of Life is a Christian-based community initiative dedicated to supporting vulnerable seniors in Zombo District, Uganda. We ensure that the elderly are seen, safe, and loved.

Through regular home visits, in-home care, hygiene support, weekly food packages, and monthly gatherings where we share a warm meal, we bring dignity, compassion, and hope to the elderly in our community.

How We Help

Home Visits & Care

Weekly visits offering companionship, hygiene support, bedding cleaning, and basic care.

Health & Welfare Support

Clinic referrals, wound care follow-up, medication reminders, and urgent case response.

Spiritual & Emotional Care

Prayer, encouragement, and counseling that restore hope and community connection.
