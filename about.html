Our Mission & Story

We believe in a world where every elder is treated with dignity, respect, and care.

Our Story

Spring of Life is a Christian-based community initiative dedicated to supporting vulnerable seniors in Zombo District, Uganda. At Spring of Life, we ensure that the elderly are seen, safe, and loved.

Through regular home visits, in-home care, hygiene support, weekly food packages, and monthly gatherings where we share a warm meal, we bring dignity, compassion, and hope to the elderly in our community.

Our work is inspired by the words of Jesus in Matthew 25:40: "Truly I tell you, whatever you did for one of the least of these brothers and sisters of mine, you did for me."

About the Founder

Spring of Life Elderly Care was founded by a compassionate individual based in Sweden, whose heart remains deeply connected to the elderly in Zombo District, Uganda.

Guided by faith and love, the founder envisioned a project that would bring care, dignity, and hope to those often forgotten. Though choosing to remain private, the founder continues to support and guide the mission from afar, working closely with local volunteers and partners to ensure the elderly are cared for with compassion and respect.
